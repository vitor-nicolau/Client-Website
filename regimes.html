Alpha - German Shepherd

Alpha is a 6 month old puppy, who loves to play with his green squeaky snake toy. He is very vocal and loves to have conversations with his owners.

Due to his breed, Alpha has a higher chance of suffering from hip dysplasia so his work out exercises have to be carefully thought out to aid him to grow into a healthy strong adult.

Alpha's Walking Plan

1 Hour Morning Walk:

  • 10 Minute Warm-up Walk
  • 30 Minute Medium-intensity Run
  • 10 Minute Play Activities
  • 10 Minute Cool-down

1 Hour Evening Walk:

  • 20 Minute Warm-up Walk
  • 20 Minute Medium-intensity Run
  • 10 Minute Play Activities
  • 10 Minute Cool-down

Blue - Siberian Husky

Blue is 3 years old and will run for hours without getting tired. He is also the world's best escape artist! At home he is very friendly and super laid back.

Huskies are notorious for being sled dogs and were bred for their strength, and due to this, we plan Blue's walks to be as high intensity as possible whilst mentally engaging him with lots of different activities to ensure all his needs are being met.

Blue's Walking Plan

1 Hour Morning Walk:

  • 5 Minute Gradual Warm-Up
  • 35 Minute High Intensity Run
  • 10 Minute Play Activities
  • 10 Minute Cool Down

1 Hour Evening Walk:

  • 5 Minute Gradual Warm-Up
  • 30 Minute Play Activities
  • 5 Minute Medium Intensity Run
  • 20 Minute Cool Down

Kasey - Yorkshire Terrier

Althought small in size Kasey is big in personality. She makes a feisty but loving companion.

Kasey's Walking Plan

1 Hour Afternoon Walk:

  • 20 Minute Low Intensity Run
